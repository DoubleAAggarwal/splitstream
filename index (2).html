The deal is yours

Three ways pairs usually work

We make the intro, you make the deal. These are the three structures that come up most, but nothing stops you from inventing your own.

DEAL TYPE 01

Rev share

The clipper keeps a percentage of what the clip accounts earn from creator funds and ad revenue. Zero upfront cost, both sides win when clips perform.

DEAL TYPE 02

Flat fee

A fixed amount per clip or per week. Simple, predictable, and clippers know exactly what they're earning regardless of views.

DEAL TYPE 03

Exposure trade

No money at all. The clipper grows their own TikTok or Shorts page using your content, you get free promotion. Common for brand-new clippers building a portfolio.

Fair questions

Asked and answered

Is it actually free?

Yes. Free for streamers, free for clippers, and we take zero percent of whatever deal you make. There's no premium tier and no card field anywhere on this site. Money, if any changes hands at all, moves directly between streamer and clipper.

What kind of deal do I have to offer?

Whatever you want. Rev share, a flat fee per clip, or a pure exposure trade where no money moves at all. You tell us what you're offering when we email you, and we only introduce you to people on the other side who are open to that kind of deal.

How is this different from Whop clipping campaigns?

Whop campaigns pay per view, which requires a budget and attracts hundreds of clippers per bounty. Splitstream is built for the segment below that: small streamers with no marketing spend, and clippers who'd rather have a direct relationship than fight a crowd. Different deal structures, different audience, no fees.

What happens after I sign up?

You get an email from us asking a few questions: what you stream or clip, your schedule, and what kind of deal you're looking for. When there's a fit on the other side, we introduce you both over email and step out of the way.

How do I end up in the directory?

When we email you after signup, we ask if you want a public listing. Say yes and you're added with your display name and what you're looking for. Your email is never shown. Say no and you're matched privately like everyone else.

What stops people from botting views?

Mostly the deal structures themselves. Rev share and exposure trades give botted views no value, since fake views earn no real revenue and grow no real audience. If you agree on per-view payment with someone, that's between you, and we'd suggest starting small until trust is built.
